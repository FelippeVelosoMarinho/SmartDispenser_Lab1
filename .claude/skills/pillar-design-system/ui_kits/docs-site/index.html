Quickstart

Get your Pillar dispenser talking to the companion app in about twenty minutes. You'll need a flashed device, a Wi-Fi network, and a phone.

Before you begin
If you haven't flashed firmware yet, start with Flashing firmware and come back here. This guide assumes a device on first boot.

1. Power on the device

Plug Pillar into its power adapter. The top ring breathes blue for ten seconds while it boots, then glows a steady blue. That's your signal to continue.

2. Open the companion app

On your phone, open Pillar and tap Connect a dispenser. You'll see a six-digit code on your device.

STEP A
Tap Connect
Bottom of the Setup screen.
STEP B
Enter the code
The six digits on the device screen.

3. Join your Wi-Fi

Pillar prefers 2.4 GHz networks for range. Pick yours from the list and enter the password. Connection usually takes under 30 seconds.

# Optional: connect over SSH for debugging
ssh pillar@pillar.local
sudo journalctl -u pillar-dispenser -f

4. Add your first medication

Back in the app, tap Add medication. Enter the name, dose, and schedule. Pillar will ask you to pour the pills into a chamber and confirm the count.

Double-check the count
Pillar trusts what you tell it. If the pill count is off, the refill reminder will be off too.

That's it.

Your first dose will dispense at the scheduled time. If you want to pull a reading from the sensors, see API reference.
