Built from the ground up to be kind.

We started from a simple premise: the software around medication should never make you feel worse about taking it.

Warm, never alarming

A missed dose is not a crisis. Pillar adjusts gently and keeps the people you've chosen in the loop.

Accessible to the bone

Color, shape, and word — every signal uses all three. Reduced-motion, high-contrast, and screen reader support ship by default.

Yours, privately

Self-host it. Run it on a Raspberry Pi. Your medication data never leaves your home unless you say so.

Made for real lives.

Pillar is designed with — not for — people with ADHD, autism, memory differences, low vision, tremor, and the caregivers who walk alongside them.

Cognitive load comes first

One thing at a time. No dashboards of numbers you have to interpret. Just: what's next, and is it ready.

Forgiving by design

Oversized targets. No pinch-to-zoom required. Every destructive action can be undone.

Caregivers, not surveillance

You choose what your people see. They never see what you didn't share.

Hardware that's repairable

Standard motors, 3D-printable chambers, commodity boards. If a part breaks, you fix it.

Build with us.

Pillar is community-built. Contributors welcome — hardware, software, translation, access testing.
